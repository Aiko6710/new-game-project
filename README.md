# City Escape

## 🎮 Overview

**City Escape** is a 2D exploration and adventure game developed using the **Godot Engine**. The game invites players to explore the rich culture, history, landmarks, and environment of **Negros Occidental, Philippines**, while navigating through challenges and interactive locations.

## 🏢 Development Information

* **Engine:** Godot Engine
* **Genre:** Exploration / Adventure
* **Platform:** PC (expandable to other platforms)
* **Project Type:** Indie / Student Game Project

## 🌏 Game Concept

City Escape is centered around exploring the cultural, historical, and geographical identity of **Negros Occidental**. Players experience different environments inspired by real towns, traditions, famous landmarks, and historical influences while progressing through the game world.

The game combines exploration with light challenges, encouraging players to learn more about the culture, history, and lifestyle of the region.

## 🏛️ History, Famous Places, and Landmarks

Negros Occidental is a province in the Philippines known for its rich history, sugar industry heritage, and beautiful landmarks.

Some inspirations for the game include:

* 🏰 **The Ruins (Talisay City)** – A famous ancestral mansion often called the "Taj Mahal of Negros" due to its romantic history and architecture.
* ⛪ **San Sebastian Cathedral (Bacolod City)** – A historic church and one of the oldest religious landmarks in the province.
* 🌳 **Capitol Park and Lagoon (Bacolod City)** – A popular public park featuring sculptures and a large lagoon, often used for relaxation and cultural events.
* 🌿 **Gawahon Eco-Park (Victorias City)** – A natural eco-park known for its lush forests, hiking trails, waterfalls, and scenic mountain environment.

These locations represent the cultural identity, history, and natural beauty of Negros Occidental, which are reflected in the game world.

## 🎯 Gameplay Objective

The main objective is to explore different areas of Negros Occidental, discover cultural and historical points of interest, and complete exploration-based tasks while progressing through the map.

## 🕹️ Key Features

* Open exploration of culturally inspired environments
* Locations based on Negros Occidental landmarks and history
* Interactive exploration-based gameplay
* Smooth and responsive controls
* Lightweight and beginner-friendly design

## 🧠 Game Design Concept

The game is designed to promote cultural and historical awareness of Negros Occidental. It focuses on exploration, discovery, and environmental storytelling rather than combat or high-intensity action.

## 🚀 How to Play

1. Launch the game via Godot Engine or exported build
2. Control the player character to explore the environment
3. Interact with objects and locations inspired by Negros Occidental
4. Complete exploration goals to progress through the game

## 🛠️ Development Notes

This project was developed as a learning experience in game development using Godot Engine. It focuses on exploration mechanics, cultural representation, and level design inspired by real-world Philippine locations.
